Importance of Vanilla JS, HTML, CSS

What is Vanilla JS

Vanilla JS means regular Javascript, without any libraries or abstractions called frameworks. It is used straight out of box along with HTML and CSS. Nowadays we are flooded with Javascript libraries and frameworks. A beginner might not know difference between frameworks and libraries and for small toy apps, they start using shiny frameworks. A good developer should know when to use what.

Why it is important to learn Vanilla JS

Though frameworks and libraries makes our life much easier. One needs to know plain javascript to appreciate frameworks and the abstraction they provide. Truth about frameworks is that they come and go. Just 4-5 years back, JQuery was the rage, now Angular, ReactJS, VueJS is topping charts. What is common in them is the use of javacript,html and css. So if you learn vanilla js, html and css, you can pick up any framework with ease.

Conclusion

We are not against any framework, but before jumping to any, one should take a look at some plain old javascript.
